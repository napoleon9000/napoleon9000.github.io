Troubleshooting

Notifications Not Working

Solution: Check your device notification settings and ensure CYY has permission to send notifications. For critical reminders, make sure "Allow Critical Alerts" is enabled in iOS Settings > Notifications > CYY.

Photos Not Saving

Solution: Verify that CYY has camera and photo library permissions in your device settings. Go to Settings > Privacy > Camera and ensure CYY is enabled.

Cloud Sync Issues

Solution: Ensure you're connected to the internet and logged into your account. Try refreshing the sync in Settings > Cloud Backup. If conflicts appear, use the conflict resolution dialog to choose the correct data.

QR Code Scanner Not Working

solution: Make sure CYY has camera permissions and you're in a well-lit environment. Clean your camera lens and try holding the QR code steady within the scanning frame.

Friend Invitations Not Working

Solution: Check your internet connection and ensure both users have the latest version of the app. Invite codes expire after 7 days, so generate a new one if needed.

App Crashes or Freezes

Solution: Force close the app and restart it. If the problem persists, try restarting your device. Make sure you have the latest version of the app installed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is my health data secure?

A: Yes! All your health data is stored locally on your device and encrypted during cloud backup. We use industry-standard security practices and never share your personal health information.

Q: Can I use the app without creating an account?

A: Absolutely! You can use CYY completely offline without any account. Creating an account is only required for social features and cloud backup.

Q: How many medications can I track?

A: There's no limit! You can add as many medications as you need, each with their own schedule, reminders, and settings.

Q: Can I set different reminder times for different days?

A: Yes! Each medication can have a flexible schedule with different times for different days of the week.

Q: What happens if I miss a notification?

A: CYY will automatically send retry notifications based on your settings (up to 99 times with customizable intervals). You can also mark medications as taken or skipped later in the app.

Q: Can I share my medication data with my doctor?

A: While CYY doesn't directly integrate with healthcare systems, you can export your medication history and compliance data to share with your healthcare provider.

Q: Is the app available in other languages?

A: Yes! CYY supports English, Simplified Chinese, and Traditional Chinese, with automatic detection of your device's language preference.

Getting Started

Setting Up Your First Medication

  • Tap the "+" button on the Home screen
  • Enter your medication name and choose a color
  • Set your reminder times and days
  • Configure retry notifications (optional)
  • Save and start receiving reminders!

Adding Friends for Support

  • Go to the Friends tab
  • Tap "Add Friend" and choose QR code or invite link
  • Share the code/link with your friend
  • Once connected, you can share medications and send reminders

Using Photo Evidence

  • When you receive a medication reminder, tap "Photo"
  • Take a picture of your medication or yourself taking it
  • The photo will be saved with a timestamp for your records
  • View your photo history in the Track screen

Privacy Policy

CYY Medication Reminder is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect minimal data necessary for app functionality and never sell or share your personal health information with third parties.

Data We Collect:

  • Medication names, schedules, and reminder settings (stored locally)
  • Photos you choose to take (stored locally)
  • Account information if you choose to create an account (for social features)
  • Anonymous usage analytics to improve the app

Data Usage:

  • All medication data is stored primarily on your device
  • Cloud backup is optional and encrypted
  • Social features require account creation and secure data sharing
  • We never access your medication data without your explicit consent
